To Make the Real Fruit Smoothie, it will be necessary to have the following ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 5 Strawberries
  • 10 Rasberries
  • 1 Cup Apple Juice
  • 1 Cup Greek Vanilla Yogurt
  • 1 1/2 Cups Ice

Mix all the ingredients together in a blender, blend until all ingredients are blended evenly.

Terminology

Blender

a thing that mixes things together, in particular an electric mixing machine used in food preparation for liquefying, chopping, or pureeing

Greek Yogurt

a type of yogurt that has been strained to remove the whey, giving it a characteristically thick consistency

Fruit

he sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seed and can be eaten as food
