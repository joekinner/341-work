Lets Make A Smoothie

To Make the Real Fruit Smoothie, it will be necessary to have the following ingredients:froven fruit works too, although fresh fruit is reccomended!

  • 1 Banana
  • 5 Strawberries
  • 10 Rasberries
  • 1 Cup Apple Juice
  • 1 Cup Greek Vanilla Yogurt
  • 1 1/2 Cups Ice

Mix all the ingredients together in a blender, blend until all ingredients are blended evenly.
